About the Dragon Queen

The Dragon Queen rules the celestial realms, her wings shimmering like moonlight across mountains and valleys. This visual story blends parallax effects with modern web design techniques to create an immersive scrolling experience. As you scroll down, the elements on the screen shift position, creating depth and motion that follow your movement.

Parallax scrolling enhances user engagement by adding visual interest without requiring extra interaction. It works across modern browsers and devices, making it a popular choice for portfolios, landing pages, and storytelling websites.
